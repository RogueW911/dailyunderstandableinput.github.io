story:
Every morning, Leo opened his tiny cafe at seven.
He did not make coffee first. He fed a street cat first.
One rainy Monday, the cat walked into the cafe and sat on the cash register.
A customer laughed and said, "Your manager is here."
Leo put a small paper hat on the cat.
The next day, three new customers came to see the famous manager.
By Friday, the cat had a name, a chair, and more fans than Leo.
Leo still made the coffee, but everyone knew who really ran the cafe.
